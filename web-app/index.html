Madam Agnes Pomaa"Auntie Bee"

"A life so beautifully lived, a love so deeply felt.
Gone from our sight, but never from our hearts."

"I am the resurrection and the life. Whoever believes in me, though he die, yet shall he live."

John 11:25

Madam Agnes Pomaa, affectionately known as Auntie Bee, was a beloved mother, grandmother, sister, and friend. Her warmth, laughter, and unwavering faith touched every life she encountered. She is dearly missed but forever remembered.
